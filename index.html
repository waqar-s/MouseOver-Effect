Turning Pixels into Perfection

Frontend Developer &
Web UI UX Designer

Hey there! My name is Waqar Baloch and I'm a web developer with a passion for creating seamless, captivating user experiences. I'm also a bit of a design nerd, and I love using tools like Adobe XD, Figma, Canva, and Spline to bring my designs to life.

My journey in web development started back in 2021 when I decided to try editing custom Tumblr themes. I quickly realized that hacking together a custom reblog button taught me a lot about HTML and CSS. From there, I fell in love with the process of building things for the web.

Today, I'm focused on building accessible, inclusive products and digital experiences at Upwork and Fiverr for a variety of clients. I'm passionate about creating experiences that are not only visually stunning, but also intuitive and engaging for everyone.

I'm a professional, but I'm also real and friendly. I love getting to know my clients and their businesses, and I'm always up for a challenge. If you're looking for a web developer who can help you create something truly special, I'm your guy!
